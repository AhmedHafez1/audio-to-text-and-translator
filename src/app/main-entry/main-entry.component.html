Welcome to Speechy!

Speechy is the ultimate app for transcribing and synthesizing speech. With Speechy, you can:

  • Transcribe speech to text in real time using Google's powerful speech recognition technology
  • Synthesize text to speech in various languages and voices using Google's natural sounding text-to-speech engine
  • Edit your transcriptions with ease using our intuitive editor
  • Save and manage your transcription history with our cloud storage
